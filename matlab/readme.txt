DESCRIPTION:
MATLAB algorithm that generates helicoidal phase masks, displays them in a transmission/reflection SLM and acquires
images of the obtained vortex intensity patterns of a digital coronagraphy system.
It is also possible to simulate the free-space propagation of two input starts (gaussian beams) and see the output.

https://www.wikiwand.com/en/README
